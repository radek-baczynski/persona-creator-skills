# Meet the cast

These are **sample personas** — real-feeling people invented after browsing actual product sites. They are not something you install from this folder; they are here so you can see what `persona-creator` produces when it works.

Think of each file as a **borrowed point of view**: open one, ask your agent to “react as this person” to a landing page, and you get objections, hooks, and gut reactions instead of generic marketing advice.

## Why we built this

We’re bootstrappers building [Transcriptmate](https://transcriptmate.com) — transcription and repurposing for people who live on recordings. We wanted honest feedback on our positioning and landing pages without booking a room full of strangers. So we used AI to stand in for plausible buyers: crawl the site, invent personas with real objections, and argue with our copy before real users do.

Most of the personas here started that way — especially [persona-solo-creator](personas/persona-solo-creator/), who reflects how we pressure-tested Transcriptmate for creators. We open-sourced `persona-creator` so you can do the same for your product.

| [persona-lean-content-marketer](personas/persona-lean-content-marketer/) | Turns founder interviews and webinars into pipeline content on a small team. |
| [persona-ux-research-lead](personas/persona-ux-research-lead/) | Needs fast, trustworthy transcripts from interviews and usability sessions. |
| [persona-focus-group-agency](personas/persona-focus-group-agency/) | Runs client research at volume; cares about repeatable analysis and deliverables. |
| [persona-independent-journalist](personas/persona-independent-journalist/) | Deadline-driven; wants quotes, structure, and takeaways from recordings fast. |
| [persona-public-speaker](personas/persona-public-speaker/) | Repurposes talks and panels into follow-up content and talks. |
| [persona-lecturer](personas/persona-lecturer/) | Records lessons and needs transcripts, summaries, and student-facing materials. |
| [persona-lawyer-client-analysis](personas/persona-lawyer-client-analysis/) | Reviews sensitive conversations; accuracy, speakers, and privacy matter most. |
| [persona-psychologist-interview-analysis](personas/persona-psychologist-interview-analysis/) | Clinical or research interviews; nuance and careful wording over speed theater. |
| [persona-phone-call-reviewer](personas/persona-phone-call-reviewer/) | Sales, advisory, or support calls — wants action items and what was actually agreed. |

Each `SKILL.md` includes a short bio, what they want, what annoys them, and what on a website would earn or lose their trust.

## Try one in conversation

Paste a URL and say something like:

> Read `examples/personas/persona-solo-creator/SKILL.md` and tell me how Sam would react to https://example.com/pricing

Or copy a persona into your agent’s skills folder if you want it to stick around — details are in the [main README](../README.md).

## Want your own cast?

Point `persona-creator` at **your** site. It will crawl, propose personas grounded in what it saw, wait for your thumbs-up, then write new `persona-*` skills tailored to your product — people who might actually buy, churn, or recommend you.
